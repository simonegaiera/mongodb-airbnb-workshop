## 📚 Two Workshop Scenarios Available

This workshop offers **two different learning experiences**:

### 🎨 **Vibe Coding** 
*Build from scratch with creative freedom*
- Start with OpenAPI specifications and requirements
- Implement the complete API solution independently
- Perfect for experienced developers who enjoy creative problem-solving
- Full autonomy in implementation choices and architectural decisions

### 📖 **Guided Exercises**
*Step-by-step structured learning*
- Follow detailed, progressive exercises
- Learn MongoDB concepts through hands-on guided activities
- Ideal for developers new to MongoDB or those who prefer structured learning
- Clear instructions and checkpoints throughout the journey

> **👨‍💼 Important**: Your **Solutions Architect (SA)** will inform you which scenario your workshop follows. Please check with them to understand which instructions and materials to use for your specific session.
